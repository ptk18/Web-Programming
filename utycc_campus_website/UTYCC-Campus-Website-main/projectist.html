ျမန္မာနွင့္ ပရဟိတ

Introduction

ျမန္မာနွင့္ပရဟိတ website တြင္ ျမန္မာနိုင္ငံ အတြင္ရွိပညာေရး၊ က်န္းမာေရး၊ မိဘမဲ့ကေလးမ်ား၊ သက္ႀကီး ႐ြယ္အိုဘိုးဘြားမ်ား၊ သဘာဝေဘးဒဏ္သင့္ေဒသမ်ားအတြင္းရွိ ပရဟိတလွဳပ္ရွားမွဳမ်ား၊ အကူအညီလိုအပ္ေနေသးေသာ ေနရာမ်ား၊ ဆက္သြယ္လွဴဒါန္းနိုင္ရန္ ပရဟိတ အဖြဲ႕အစည္းမ်ား၏ လိပ္စာမ်ားအားတင္ျပထားပါသည္။

Aims and Objective

  • ပရဟိတဆိုေသာ စကားလံုးကိုနားလည္ေစရန္ျဖစ္သည္။ ပရဟိတ ဆိုတာဘာလဲ၊ ပရဟိတလုပ္လွ်င္လည္း လိုက္နာရမည့္အခ်က္မ်ားကို သိရွိေစရန္ ရည္႐ြယ္ျခင္းျဖစ္သည္
  • ကၽြန္ေတာ္၊ မ တို႔ျမန္မာနိုင္ငံ အတြင္း အမွန္တကယ္လိုအပ္ေနေသာ ေနရာကို သိရွိေစလိုေသာ ရည္႐ြယ္ခ်က္ျဖစ္သည္၊ ပရဟိတနွင့္ မရင္းနွီးသည့္သူမ်ားကိုလည္း ပါဝင္လာေစရန္ ရည္႐ြယ္သည္
  • အခ်င္းခ်င္းပါဝင္ကူညီလာျခင္းျဖင့္ ကုသိုလ္တရားပြားမ်ားကာ၊ ကူညီရိုင္းပင္းတတ္ေသာ စိတ္ပိုင္းဆိုင္ရာအေလ့အက်င့္မ်ားေပၚေပါက္လာေစရန္ျဖစ္သည္၊ ပိုမိုေကာင္းမြန္ေသာ အနာဂတ္မ်ားလည္းရရွိလာမည္ျဖစ္သည္

Car Insurance Management System

Introduction

Car Insurance Management System is basically for management of customers who take insurance for their cars. In this system, all records are maintained in the database. It is able to be used in Car Insurance Department from any Insurance Company to manage for their customers’ insurance details.

Aims and Objective

  • To save time consuming and power consuming
  • To provide more reliability for maintaining information
  • To have more storage capacity and search facility and faster access to database
  • To be able to calculate premium easier rather than calculating by manual

Learning English for Life

Introduction

By learning courses, reading books at library, studying skill improvement and testing exam at the English learning for life, you can improve your English knowledge and skills. Users can choose the courses (Basic, Intermediate 1, Intermediate 2, and Advanced), and study the units, practice the questions about the respective level that you choose. By testing the exams, the users can know your acquisition about you learn and touching the placement test, the users can know your level. When the users are learning for a long time, they can feel boring. So, the users can read the story books about English at that system. And the user can get the ways how to improve your skills (Listening, Reading, Writing, Grammar and Speaking) by studying skill improvement. Moreover, the user can review the records about they studied.

Aims and Objective

  •  To enable you to improve your ability
  •  To provide “Effective English Lessons” with no waste of time
  •  To help teaching English Lessons for Schools and English Courses
  •  To support “Active Learning” with the help of technology
  •  To provide each learner’s skill accurately with effective questions
