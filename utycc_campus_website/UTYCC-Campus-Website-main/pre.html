Precision Engineering ( PrE )

Precision engineering and manufacturing issues are becoming ever more important in current and future technologies. Precision engineering is defined as painstaking attention to details and requires knowledge of a wide variety of measurement, fabrication, and control issues. Precision manufacturing is defined as the manufacture of individual pieces with extreme accuracy. This type of machining is used to make parts for various machines, including medical, aeronautical, and any other industry that requires identical parts to be created in large quantities.Precision engineering is a sub-discipline of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, software engineering, electrical engineering, control engineering, and optical engineering. It is concerned with designing machines, fixture, and other structures that have exceptionally high tolerances, are repeatable, and are stable over time. This engineering field will aid in the advancement of various technologies that need to gain industrial competitiveness.
