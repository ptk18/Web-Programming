❝ Yearly Activiy Plan ❞

































Fresher Welcome

This is the welcome party for newcomers to the university by their senior Students. In this party, all students of our university takes place actively and the fantastic parformance of the students can be found.


Celebrate in ~ January





Independent Day

In independent day, street games like running, tug of war, food eating races and sports like badminton, tennis are held together in front of main building. Independent day is supposed to be about unity, and appears to bring ethic communities together.


Celebrate in ~ January





Blood Donation

A voluntary unpaid blood donation is held in this day. Some students donate blood and some perform as volunteers. Some nutritious food like breads and eggs are returned back to the donater. The blood donation is held twice a year.


Celebrate in ~ January/August





Kahtain

This is the ceremony of donating Kahtain Robes to Buddhist Monks, and the instumental and expressive nature of our university can be found. You can also find the creative skill to make the Padatharpin.


Celebrate in ~ January





YCC Invent

This is the competition of different years & majors of our university. The innovation of our ycc students is breathtaking.


Celebrate in ~ February





Convocation

Last years students who finished their seminors and thisis for the degree are awarded bachaler degrees at the convocation ceremony.


Celebrate in ~ February





Htamanae

In the ceremony, the contestants cook Htamanae, a mixture of Glutinous Rice, fried coconut shavings, roasted peanuts, and other ingredents in a team. This ceremony helps promote unity and mutual support.


Celebrate in ~ February





Cyber Thingyan

This the new year festival after the mid-term exam. The tradition of Myanmar water festival can be found there. All the students play water happily and peacefully.


Celebrate in ~ April





Red Cross Training

This is the camp of first aid and can get the volunteering mind-set and teamwork. Firsr aid in this camp is lectured by the coach from the Myanmar Red Cross Society.


Celebrate in ~ June





Rainy Season Sport Activities

In rainy season, sport matches like football, badminton, tennis, cane ball are held to become healthy and intelligent students .


Celebrate in ~ June/July





Warso

This is the ceremony of donating Warso Robes to Buddhist Monks, and the instumental and expressive nature of our university can be found. Our university donate money and warso robes to the near monestries.


Celebrate in ~ July





Literature Talks

The invited authors discuss fields of literature or information techonology in the talk to improve knowledge and thinking power. All of the students attend the talk and they listen and discuss to the authors.


Celebrate in ~ July





Project Show

Students apply their knowledge from their acadamic learning by making projects. These different projects are showned together there and they share about their projects to the visitors to the show. This is the famous tradition of our university and the innovation and the creation of the student are unbelievable.


Celebrate in ~ August





Sayar Gadaw Pwe & Awarding Ceremony

Students and some alumni of the university pay respect to their teachers in this ceremony. And the awarding ceremony to the outstanding students is held together in this day.


Celebrate in ~ September





Night Of YCC

This is the night party that crowded with night market of food that is no-paid and people who play firework, pyrotechnic and hot-air balloon.Famous singers and music bands are invited and relax after the final-exam.


Celebrate in ~ October
